About the Project

Revolutionizing Liver Care is a forward-thinking healthcare project aimed at improving the early detection and treatment of liver cirrhosis using cutting-edge machine learning technologies.

Visual comparison between a healthy liver and one affected by cirrhosis. Source: Healthline

Cirrhosis is a progressive liver condition often diagnosed at later stages. By analyzing clinical, lifestyle, and demographic data, our models—such as Random Forest, Naïve Bayes, XGBoost, Logistic Regression, and Support Vector Machines—deliver accurate predictions to assist doctors in timely intervention.

The project empowers healthcare professionals with intelligent, data-driven tools to support better clinical decisions, reduce diagnostic delays, and improve patient outcomes. It represents a significant step toward predictive and personalized medicine.
